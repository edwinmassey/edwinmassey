Describing copyright and other constraints affecting websites

What is copyright?

Copyright protection is established to protect copyright owners from having other people use their materials without their permission. This can include materials such as images, text, music, graphics and website layout. In order to have copyright protection it must be your own original piece of work.

Breaking copyright laws

When creating a website it is important not to infringe copyright laws as breaking this law can result in a large fine being paid or in some cases even jail time. Also breaking this law can result in users being skeptical about your work as they may assume that you have copied other materials in your work.
